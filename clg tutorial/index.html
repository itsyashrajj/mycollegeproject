B.Sc

Bachelor of Science (Bsc full form) that is a three to five years undergraduate academic degree. BSc degree is awarded for various subjects, sometimes even for non-scientific subjects. Different countries award Bsc degrees differently. In most cases, science students pursue Bachelors of Science.

BCA

The term BCA stands for Bachelor of Computer Applications. A three-year undergraduate degree in technology and computer applications is known as a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA). This is the one of the most popular technical degree among students called professional degree.

M.Sc

An MSc (Master of Science) degree is a popular postgraduate degree that students mostly choose after MSc. An MSc is for those who want to improve their skills in order to move up in their career field. It is the postgraduate degree which was mainly done after the BSc course by the students.
